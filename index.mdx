The Telescope Net is an accessible astronomy platform that lets anyone contribute real science to professional databases. Donate your Seestar telescope's nights to the network; the pipeline handles everything — scheduling, photometry, and AAVSO submission — under your name. No dark-site trips, no manual controls, no expertise required.

## How the network works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the Node Agent">
    Download the one-click installer for Windows, macOS, or Linux. It configures OS sleep prevention and registers your Seestar automatically on first boot.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive your Activation Code">
    Create a member account and request a Node Activation Code (`BS-YYYY-XXXXXXXX`). Paste it into `config.yaml` — the node registers itself on the first heartbeat.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The network takes over every night">
    At astronomical twilight the Node Agent downloads tonight's observation plan, controls your Seestar via ALPACA, runs on-device photometry on each FITS file, and uploads measurements to the cloud.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your data reaches AAVSO">
    The cloud cross-validates measurements across nodes, runs quality gates, and submits accepted photometry to AAVSO WebObs under your observer code. You receive a night-summary notification each morning.
  </Step>
</Steps>
